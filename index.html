Visit San Jose

Enjoy culture in this vibrant city located in the heart of California.

Top Three San Jose Activities

Santana Row

This 42-acre area is called the “Village within a City,”
and is home to 50 shops, 30 restaurants, and residential areas.

Winchester Mystery House

A peculiar mansion built by the troubled heir to
the Winchester gun fortune.

San Jose Heritage Rose Garden

You will encounter over 3,600 different rose bushes
representing more than 2,700 different varieties at this garden.
